Mojito

Cocktail

Havana, Cuba, is the birthplace of the mojito, although its exact origin is the subject of debate. The mojito is one of the most famous rum-based highballs.

Mojito Recipe:

  • 45ml white rum
  • 20ml lime juice
  • 10ml sugar syrup
  • mint
  • soda water
