Japanese Food & Beverages

Sushi

A Japanese dish consisting of small balls or rolls of vinegar-flavoured cold rice served with a garnish of vegetables, egg, or raw seafood.

The most popular sushi varieties are:

  • Nigiri
  • Sashimi
  • Maki
  • Uramaki
  • Temaki

Green Tea

Japanese tea is a beverage known worldwide for being both healthy and delicious. The country's history with tea goes back more than 1,000 years.

The most popular japanese tea varieties are:

  • Sencha
  • Matcha
  • Hojicha
  • Gyokuro
  • Genmaicha
