Anime & Manga

Anime

Anime is one of the most popular forms of animation in Japan. It is used to present stories with different themes, characters and settings. The wide range of genres in anime is the first reason why it is so popular.

Some of my favorite animes are:

  • Death Note
  • Code Geass
  • Dragon Ball Z
  • Gundam
  • Demon Slayer

Manga

Manga is a term used in Japan for comics or graphic novels. Most manga conform to a style developed in Japan in the late 19th century, and the form has a long prehistory in earlier Japanese art. The term manga is used in Japan to refer to both comics and cartooning.

Mangas that I would recommend:

  • Berserk
  • One Puch Man
  • Attack On Titan
